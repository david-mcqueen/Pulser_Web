Hear your Heart.

Pulser is an audible heart rate monitor, connecting directly to your Bluetooth (BLE) device to provide you constant updates as to how hard you are pushing your heart. Know when you are at your peak, and know when you can give that little bit extra and set a new personal record. Hear your Heart, and reach your training goals.

Download now for iPhone

Free! on the app store

Features

HealthKit

Save your heart rate directly to HealthKit. Set custom interval times for how often your heart rate is saved

Audio Announcements

Your heart rate is read aloud, at custom interval times set by you. Know exactly how hard you are working your heart

Training Zones

Set custom training zones, tailoring Pulser to your heart

Zone Calculator

Simply enter your age and resting heart rate, let Pulser do the rest and calculate your training zones. Coming Soon.

Stand Alone

Works independently of other applications, providing you with accurate heart rate measurements whether you are in the gym or out for a run

Integration

Integrates flawlessly with other health tracking apps, such as Strava
